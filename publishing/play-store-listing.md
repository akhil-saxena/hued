## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Every photo you take carries color. Over weeks and months, those colors tell a story you've never seen: muted greys through a quiet winter, vibrant greens from a trip abroad, warm amber from evenings at home.

hued reveals that story.

Open the app and your current week's palette is already waiting. Five dominant colors extracted from your photos, ordered by when they appeared, with a poetic one-liner that captures the mood. Scroll to see your color history across weeks, months, and years.

Your palette strip is more than data. It's a fingerprint of how you see the world.

WHAT MAKES HUED DIFFERENT

Privacy is structural, not a setting. All color extraction happens on your device. Your photos never leave your phone. No accounts, no cloud, no analytics.

The output is inherently beautiful. The palette strip IS the visual. No decoration needed, no filters applied. What you see is genuinely yours.

FEATURES

Instant palette on launch. No taps, no navigation. Your colors are waiting.

Time views. Switch between week, month, and year to see your color story at different scales.

31,000+ color names. Every color gets an evocative name matched by perceptual distance in CIELAB color space.

Poetic descriptions. Each palette gets a one-line description matched to its warmth, saturation, and lightness. 270 handwritten descriptions across 27 mood categories.

Share cards. Beautiful 9:16 story cards with your palette strip, poetic text, and top colors. Preview before sharing. Save to gallery or share directly.

Smart folder management. Auto-excludes screenshots, WhatsApp media, and downloads. Customizable in settings.

Weekly notification. Monday at 9 AM: "Your week in color is ready." Only fires when you've taken enough photos.

Home screen widget. Your current palette, always visible.

Settings that matter. Palette depth (3-15 colors), weighted color bands, folder selection. Changes trigger a full reprocess.

HOW IT WORKS

hued scans your camera photos, downsamples each to 300px, and extracts dominant colors using the Android Palette API. Colors are then clustered in CIELAB color space so perceptually similar shades merge into one. The palette strip is ordered chronologically, showing your color journey through the period.

All processing runs as a foreground service with progress indication. A full gallery of 1,000+ photos processes in minutes.

DESIGNED WITH CARE

Outfit font family (Light, Regular, Bold). Neutral off-white canvas that subtly tints based on your palette. No Material Design defaults. Every pixel is intentional.

The palette strip is the interface. Everything else serves it.
